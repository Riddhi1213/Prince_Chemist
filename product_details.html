Key Features

  • 360° cleaning action reaches all areas of the mouth including teeth, tongue, cheeks, and gums
  • Multi-level bristles effectively remove plaque and food particles from hard-to-reach areas
  • Integrated tongue and cheek cleaner helps remove odor-causing bacteria for fresher breath
  • Ergonomic handle design provides comfortable grip and better control during brushing
  • Dentist recommended for comprehensive oral hygiene and cavity prevention

Usage Instructions

  1. Apply a pea-sized amount of toothpaste to the bristles
  2. Brush teeth gently in circular motions for 2 minutes
  3. Use the tongue cleaner on the back of the brush head to clean your tongue
  4. Rinse mouth thoroughly with water
  5. Clean the toothbrush and store in an upright position
  6. Replace toothbrush every 3 months or when bristles become frayed

Safety Information

  • • Not suitable for children under 12 years without adult supervision
  • • Do not share toothbrush with others to prevent cross-contamination
  • • Replace immediately if bristles become damaged or worn
  • • Consult dentist if you experience gum bleeding or sensitivity
